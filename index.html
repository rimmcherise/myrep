A Guide to Buying $Pogai

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to https://phantom.app.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $Pogai. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Rraydium.io

Connect to Raydium. Go to https://jup.ag/swap/SOL-POGAI_2miHpQbYLPvXxC2V234jrrrtnJdejd5xW883a5ToNW3g in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $Pogai token address into Raydium, select $Pogai, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for Pogai

Switch SOL for $Pogai. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
